What is the Life below Water?


Target 14.1: Reduce marine pollution:

By 2025, prevent and significantly reduce marine pollution of all kinds, in particular from land-based activities, including marine debris and nutrient pollution.


-> The "Index of Coastal Eutrophication (ICEP)" assesses river nutrient inputs (nitrogen, phosphorus, and silica) and their ratios. "Floating Plastic Debris Density" models plastic distribution in the ocean, categorizing micro (less than 4.75mm) and macro (more than 4.75cm) plastics.
-> While global plastic pollution is widespread, Goal 14 has just one indicator addressing plastic impact. Other sustainable development goals lack specific microplastic reduction targets due to data limitations, creating a significant challenge for governments in monitoring and reporting on microplastic environmental impacts.
